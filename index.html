FAQ

Common questions.

How does the free trial work? ▾

You get one full month free — no credit card required. We set up your monitoring brief, run the first scan, and you receive a real digest. After the trial, choose the plan that fits. If PlanningScout isn't delivering value, you simply don't subscribe. No awkward cancellations.

Is this only useful for appeal work? ▾

No. While appeal leads are a core use case, PlanningScout can be configured for any planning intelligence need: tracking new major applications for instruction leads, monitoring prior approvals, watching specific sites or areas, or following competitor and market activity in your sector.

Which councils are covered? ▾

50+ councils across England — Greater Manchester, West Yorkshire, London boroughs, South East, East Midlands, West Midlands, East of England, South West, and North East. Coverage expands regularly. The Practitioner plan covers up to 10 councils; Professional and Practice plans cover all available councils.

How is the lead score calculated? ▾

Each application is scored based on how strongly it matches your monitoring brief. For appeal leads, the score weights the strength of the refusal grounds — "lack of evidence" refusals score highest (easiest win on appeal), sequential test failures score strongly, and generic boilerplate is filtered out. Scoring logic is fully configurable per client.

Where does the data come from? ▾

Entirely from public UK planning portals — the same pages any member of the public can access. Application details, decision notices, and applicant names are all publicly available. We read and process that data automatically, at scale, every week. No private systems, no GDPR concerns.

What format does the digest come in? ▾

A formatted email every Monday morning, plus a live Google Sheet updated weekly with your full lead history. The sheet is searchable, sortable, and has a column for your own notes and status. Practice plan subscribers also get CSV and API export for integration into their own tools.

Can I change my monitoring brief later? ▾

Yes — your brief is a living configuration, not a one-time setup. If you expand into new sectors, add councils, or want to refine your trigger set based on what's coming through, just get in touch. We update it within 48 hours and it takes effect on the next weekly run.

What if a council I need isn't covered? ▾

Let us know during onboarding — we prioritise adding councils our users need. Most UK councils use Idox planning software, which we support. If a council uses a different system, we'll tell you honestly whether it's feasible and give you a timeline.
