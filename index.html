How it works

Three steps.
Zero manual searching.

We do the monitoring, reading, and scoring. You focus on making the calls and winning the work.

Step 01

We scan 44+ UK planning portals

Every week, PlanningScout automatically searches the planning registers of 44+ UK councils — covering Greater Manchester, West Yorkshire, London, the South East, Midlands and more. Every application that could match your brief is captured and processed.

Step 02

We read every decision notice and relevant document

For each application, PlanningScout opens the decision notice and any other relevant documents — then scans for the exact trigger phrases, keywords, and signals that define a good lead for your practice. Refused applications, approved ones, or both. Whatever your brief says.

Step 03

Your shortlist arrives every Monday

A scored, ranked digest of that week's best matches — with application reference, location, use class, description, and the trigger phrases found. Direct link to the council portal. Ready on your desk at the start of the working week.

FAQ

Common questions.

How does the free trial work? ▾

You get one full month free with no credit card required. We start with an onboarding call, configure your brief, run the first scan, and send you a real digest. After the trial, choose whether to continue at £149/month. If it's not delivering value, you simply don't subscribe — no awkward cancellations.

Is this only for appeal consultants? ▾

No. While appeal leads are a core use case, PlanningScout can be configured for any planning intelligence need — new major applications where you can offer specialist consultancy, prior approval monitoring, site-specific watching, or market intelligence in a target sector. We configure the system around what you actually do.

Which councils are covered? ▾

44+ councils across England — Greater Manchester (including Tameside, Manchester, Salford, Bolton, Bury, Rochdale, Trafford, Oldham), West Yorkshire (Leeds, Bradford, Wakefield, Calderdale, Wigan), London boroughs, South East, East Midlands, West Midlands, East of England, South West, and North East. We're adding more councils regularly. If a council you need isn't covered yet, let us know — we prioritise by demand.

How is the lead score calculated? ▾

Each application is scored based on how strongly it matches your brief. For appeal leads, the score weights the strength of the refusal grounds — "lack of evidence" refusals score highest (easiest wins on appeal), sequential test failures score strongly, and generic boilerplate citations score low. The scoring logic is fully configurable per client. Applications below your minimum threshold are never shown to you.

Where does the data come from? ▾

Entirely from public UK planning portals — the same pages any member of the public can access. Application details, decision notices, and agent names are all publicly available. We read and process that data automatically, at scale, every week. No private data systems, no GDPR concerns around what we provide to you.

What format does the digest come in? ▾

A formatted email every Monday morning, plus a live Google Sheet updated weekly with your full lead history. The sheet is searchable, sortable, and has a column for your own notes and status tracking. High-score matches also trigger an immediate email alert so nothing urgent waits until Monday.

Can I change my brief after we've set it up? ▾

Yes — your brief is a living configuration. If you expand into new sectors, want to add councils, or want to refine your trigger set based on what's coming through, just let us know. We update it within 48 hours and it takes effect on the next weekly run.

Why don't you include applicant contact details? ▾

In line with GDPR best practice, we don't include applicant names or personal contact information in your digest. We do include agent details and a direct link to the public portal page — where the full application, including any agent and applicant contact information, is publicly accessible and updated by the council.
